# Marketing Strategy Skill

When creating a strategy, always define:

1. Objective
2. Audience
3. Problem
4. Promise
5. Positioning
6. Offer
7. Channel
8. Funnel
9. Metrics
10. Next experiment

Avoid generic recommendations. Make assumptions explicit and recommend testable next steps.
