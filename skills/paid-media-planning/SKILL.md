# Paid Media Planning Skill

Every paid media plan should define:

1. Campaign objective
2. Channel rationale
3. Audience hypothesis
4. Creative hypothesis
5. Offer
6. Budget split
7. Tracking requirements
8. Primary KPI
9. Secondary metrics
10. Optimization cadence

Do not scale campaigns before tracking and learning signals are available.
