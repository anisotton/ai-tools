# Marketing Review Skill

Review outputs against:

1. Strategic alignment
2. Audience fit
3. Clarity
4. Specificity
5. Proof
6. Ethical persuasion
7. Brand consistency
8. Conversion logic
9. Measurability
10. Next action clarity

Classify the output as approved, approved with changes, or not approved.
